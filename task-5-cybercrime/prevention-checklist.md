# Stay Safe Online – UPI Fraud Prevention Checklist

## General Safety Tips
- Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone.
- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links.
- Always verify the identity of callers claiming to be from banks.
- Avoid downloading apps from untrusted sources.
- Regularly update your apps and phone software.
- Check URLs carefully before entering any personal information.

## UPI-Specific Safety Tips
- Remember: entering UPI PIN is only required for sending money, not receiving.
- Do not approve unknown "collect requests" on UPI apps.
- Never scan random QR codes shared by unknown people.

## Emergency & Reporting
- Report fraud immediately at: https://www.cybercrime.gov.in
- Call Cyber Crime Helpline: **1930**

## Smart Habits
- Enable app locks and biometric security on payment apps.
- Monitor bank statements regularly for suspicious transactions.
