# Social Media Checklist

## Do’s
- Keep your profile professional and updated  
- Share useful and meaningful content  
- Use proper language and avoid offensive words  
- Respect others' opinions and communicate politely  
- Be careful about what personal information you share  

## Don’ts
- Do not share sensitive information like passwords or OTPs  
- Avoid posting inappropriate or offensive content  
- Do not engage in arguments or online fights  
- Avoid spreading false or unverified information  
- Do not misuse social media for harmful activities
